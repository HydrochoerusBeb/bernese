Bernese Mountain Dogs

Your Loving and Gentle Companions

Overview

The Bernese Mountain Dog is a large and friendly breed known for its beautiful tri-color coat and gentle nature. Originating from the Swiss Alps, they make excellent family pets and are often used as therapy dogs.

Characteristics

  • Large and Sturdy Build
  • Tri-Color Coat (Black, White, Rust)
  • Friendly and Gentle Temperament
  • Intelligent and Trainable

Care Tips

Proper care for Bernese Mountain Dogs includes regular grooming, daily exercise, and a balanced diet. Due to their thick coat, they may need extra attention in hot weather to prevent overheating.
